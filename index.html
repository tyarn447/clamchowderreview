About My Journey

Welcome aboard! I'm Taylor Yarn, a Security Engineer by day, but my true, unbridled passion since childhood has been the majestic, creamy depths of Clam Chowder. I am on an uncompromising mission to slurp, review, and analyze the finest chowders in and around Boston, while occasionally dropping some deeply esoteric, profound thought pieces on the existential nature of the chowder experience. Whether it's dissecting the perfect clam-to-potato ratio or securing the perimeter of a bread bowl, I'm here to document every delicious, absurd drop of this voyage.
