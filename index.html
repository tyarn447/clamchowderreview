About My Journey

Live and slurp, mollusk friend. I'm Taylor Yarn, a Security Engineer by day, but my true, unbridled passion since childhood has been the majestic, creamy depths of Clam Chowder. I am on an uncompromising mission to slurp, review, and analyze the finest chowders in the ruins of Boston, while occasionally dropping some deeply esoteric, profound thought pieces on the existential nature of the chowder experience. Whether it's dissecting the perfect clam-to-potato ratio or securing the perimeter of a bread bowl from chrome idols, I'm here to document every delicious, absurd drop of this trek across the salt pan.
